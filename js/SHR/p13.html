Other Hazards Rating


Other Significant hazard can include:


  • Man-made or natural obstructions, including:
    • Rock, reefs or Groynes.
    • Jetties, outflow pipes, wreck etc.
    • Uncontrolled surf craft. EG.Ski Rating: 1 for each ski. Boat Rating: 6 per boat(for 1 boat and 5 oars).
    • Floating logs, swaweed and other flotsam.
  • Water temperature (without wetsuits): (<14OC has Hazard Rating=2);(14 to 15 OC has Hazard Rating = 1).
  • pollution, marine stinger etc.
  • Visual impairment: for example, the sun setting over the ocean



OHR:add'1' for each extra hazard present
